Maintaining a healthy body fat level:

Here are some tips for maintaining a healthy body fat level: Eat a balanced diet: Focus on whole, unprocessed foods, including fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean protein sources.
Limit unhealthy fats, added sugars, and refined carbohydrates. Engage in regular physical activity: Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic activity per week, combined with strength training exercises two or more days per week.
Consult a healthcare professional: They can help you determine a healthy body fat range for you and develop a personalized plan to achieve and maintain your goals.
